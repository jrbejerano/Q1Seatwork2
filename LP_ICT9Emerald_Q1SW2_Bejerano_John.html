CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin Also know as Ibarra he is a wealthy young man and is the son of Don Rafael Ibarra. He had studied in europe for seven years and has the dream of creating a school in san diego
Maria Clara Maria Clara was the daughter of Captian Tiago and his wife but it is later discovered that she is actually the daughter of Father Dámaso after some scandalous relation ship between Father Dámaso and Captian Tiago's wife who had died during the birth of Maraa Clara. She is also the childhood love and fiancee of Ibarra
Father Damaso Father Damaso is the curate of the San diego church. He is often discribed as brash, arrogant, power-hungry, and shamelessly corrupt. He is the god-father and biological father of Maria Clara because of this he trys to object to Maria clara and Ibbara being married.
Aunt Isabel The cousin of Capitan Tiago, she had raised Maria clara as her own child due to the death of her biological mother
Oldman Tasio Oldman Tasio is on one hand described as a philosopher and on the other he is described as crazy, he had given Ibbara and his father advice
